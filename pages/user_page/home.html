Frequently Asked Question

  • bookmark Q1 : How to join?

    Just visit our register form at here and fill all information.

  • bookmark Q2 : Who can post a job?

    All resgistered user can post a job. User can define the job criteria and the reward

  • bookmark Q3 : How much reward from job?

    The reward is depend on how hard the job is, more harder the job, the more reward will you get. If you post job make sure to give high amount of reward so you job will be accepted by other in no time.

  • bookmark Q4 : What is the benefit for joining this guild?

    You can access our forum, access in game guild skill, attending our guild event with many gift, and many more.

  • bookmark Q5 : Is there any benefit for new member?

    New member can get welcome gift of 1,000,000 spina.

  • bookmark Q6 : What is spina?

    Spina is in game currency, you can use it to buy things in game. You also can exchange it to real money with other player, but keep in mind only exchange with Black Regalia guild member to minimalize scammer.

  • bookmark Q7 : How the job sistem work?

    We know the job sistem is out of game. Here is the workflow:
    if you want to post a job:
    1. Registered user must store some amount of spina in the game, and the user account in this site will be updated with the stored spina.
    2. User can post a job with his spina as reward.
    3. After someone accepted the job, user can wait until the job is finished by them. The reward is automatically transfered to them.
    if you want to take a job:
    1. Registered user can go to job page, and select one job.
    2. complete the job.
    3. report back to this site and get the reward.
    4. exchange to in game spina.

  • bookmark Q8 : What is membership ranks?

    That is a rank for member, it's divided to iron, siver, gold, and platinum. Platinum member can take platium job, gold member can take gold job, and so on.
